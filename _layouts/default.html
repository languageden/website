Language Den Features

Flashcards

At the heart of Language Den is our flashcard creation and review system. Create flashcards with as little or as much information as you need and review them daily using our spaced repetition system.

Spaced Repetition

In order to learn new words or concepts in as little time as possible we add them to your daily reviews at the perfect time so you don't spend excessive time practicing what you already know.

Statistics

We provide you with statistics about individual flashcards and your learning trends so you can determine what you should be learning next and which areas you excell at already.

Mobile Ready

Use our IOS or Android mobile apps to take your study with you on the road. Complete your daily reviews or any other tasks while away from your computer.

Text to Speech

With Text to Speech you can easily hear how a word is meant to be pronounced in your target language. This works is individual words and for full sentences.

Personal Dictionary

As you enter your words and phrases into Language Den you will build up your own personal dictionary of words you can search and revise with ease.

Your Language Learning Dashboard

Your dashboard lets you know what study you have ready for today and how much work you have coming up soon.

You can also write down notes or review a few flashcards all on your dashboard.

Notes, Grammar and Vocabulary

As you study your new language record anything you learn so you can review and recall it later.

After all your notes are in Language Den you will be periodically reminded to review each item so you will never forget it.

Mobile Learning

Language Den works perfectly in your mobile browser so you can take notes and complete your daily reviews anywhere you go.

If you are studying in a classroom or with a teach using Language Den on your tablet is a perfect way to record your lessons.

Demo

Sign in using the demonstration account to see what Language Den is all about. In the demonstration account you will be able to see what an active account looks like and also get a preview of how you can review your flashcards.
